❌ The Campus Scheduling Challenge

The Problem

Traditional sports booking portals fail to control booking abuse, leaving physical courts empty or monopolized by specific groups.

  • Monopoly & Court Hogging: Groups blocking prime-time slots back-to-back.
  • No-Show Waste: Facilities booked but never checked into, locking out other active students.
  • Attendance Fraud: Remote logins checking in from outside the venue boundaries.
  • Teammate Matching Friction: Solo players unable to organize teams or match Student IDs.

🔒 Monopolies

Same group booking slots consecutively, preventing student rotation.

📍 Attendance Fraud

Remote check-ins checking in without being physically on court.

🎯 Team Friction

Solo players cannot easily coordinate or find missing teammates.

🌐 Language

Lack of localized interfaces and error messages in Arabic.

🌐 Dual-Language Layout Engine

Bilingual Integration

To accommodate both international and national students, the portal features a floating toggle switch that flips the entire website context.

  • Dynamic Layout Shifts: Instantly flips layout direction (LTR ↔ RTL) and dynamically applies `.rtl-mode` styles.
  • Contextual Translations: Every tab, attendance map, chatbot bubble, and form updates instantly.
  • Smart Exception Mapping: Automatically intercepts complex backend database constraints and translates them on-the-fly to readable Arabic.

📍 Geofenced Attendance Verification

Fraud Prevention

Ensures students are physically present at the sports facility during their booking time window to claim attendance points.

  • Leaflet Maps Integration: Dynamic map tracking displaying student position and geofence boundary coordinates.
  • 50m Radius Guard: The check-in button remains locked if the browser's coordinates are outside the 50m facility radius.
  • No-Show Automatic Ban: Failing to check in within 15 minutes of the slot start cancels the booking, penalizing the user. 3 violations lead to a temporary 1-week account ban.

🎯 Teammate Discovery & Anonymous Lobby

Matchmaking

Students who lack a team can join an anonymous matchmaking queue instead of leaving the court underutilized.

  • Anonymous Waiting: Queues mask user details to ensure privacy and eliminate social anxiety.
  • Automated Scheduling: The system groups balanced lists of players, reserves the court, and generates balanced teams (Team A vs Team B).
  • Teammate ID Promos: Attempts to book with participants > 1 without entering teammate IDs are blocked and display an option directing the user to the matchmaking portal.

💬 AI Assistant & Offline Knowledge

Conversational AI

Badya AI behaves as an intelligent booking concierge, supporting full natural language interactions.

  • LLM Power: Powered by Gemini APIs, answering complex questions about reservation rules, points, and warnings.
  • Automated Tool Routing: Conversational tools allow the bot to query active court listings or redirect users to booking forms.
  • Offline Fallback: If internet connections are disrupted, the bot switches to a local vector-like offline database of rules and contacts.
