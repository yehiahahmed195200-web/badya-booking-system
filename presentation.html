❌ The Campus Scheduling Challenge

The Problem

Traditional sports booking portals fail to control booking abuse, leaving physical courts empty or monopolized by specific groups.

  • Monopoly & Court Hogging: Groups blocking prime-time slots back-to-back.
  • No-Show Waste: Facilities booked but never checked into, locking out other active students.
  • Attendance Fraud: Remote logins checking in from outside the venue boundaries.
  • Teammate Matching Friction: Solo players unable to organize teams or match Student IDs.

🔒 Monopolies

Same group booking slots consecutively, preventing student rotation.

📍 Attendance Fraud

Remote check-ins checking in without being physically on court.

🎯 Team Friction

Solo players cannot easily coordinate or find missing teammates.

🌐 Language

Lack of localized interfaces and error messages in Arabic.

🌐 Dual-Language Layout Engine

Bilingual Integration

To accommodate both international and national students, the portal features a floating toggle switch that flips the entire website context.

  • Dynamic Layout Shifts: Instantly flips layout direction (LTR ↔ RTL) and dynamically applies `.rtl-mode` styles.
  • Contextual Translations: Every tab, attendance map, chatbot bubble, and form updates instantly.
  • Smart Exception Mapping: Intercepts raw Spring Boot database constraints and localizes them instantly in the UI (e.g., already booked slots show suggestions in Arabic).

⚖️ Fairness Rules & Weekly Quotas

Rotation Policy

An automated balancing engine enforces rules to prevent monopolization and optimize court utility.

Rule / Policy Threshold Limit Description
Weekly Booking Limit Max 3 Bookings / Week Resets every Monday morning at 08:00 AM automatically.
Booking Cooldown 24-Hour Buffer Prevents users from spamming court reservations.
Consecutive Slot Guard Max 2 Hours / Group Blocks bookings sharing 50%+ teammate overlap consecutively.
Priority Score Dynamic Weight Overlapping slots resolved automatically based on weekly quota debt.
💡 Fairness Objective

Prevents monopolization on the basketball/volleyball courts. If there is a slot overlap dispute, the team with the higher Priority Score wins the reservation.

Advanced Features

Combines Leaflet mapping boundaries with an online/offline chatbot companion.
