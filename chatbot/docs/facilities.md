# Badya Sports Facilities
Badya University offers a variety of world-class sports facilities for students and faculty.

## Football Pitch
- **Capacity**: 10-22 players
- **Price**: Free for students (requires booking)
- **Equipment**: Balls provided at the sports office.
- **Rules**: Proper sports attire and football boots are required.

## Tennis Courts (2 courts)
- **Capacity**: 2-4 players per court
- **Price**: Free
- **Equipment**: Rackets available for rent.
- **Rules**: Non-marking shoes only.

## Padel Court
- **Capacity**: 2-4 players
- **Price**: 50 EGP per hour (for maintenance)
- **Note**: Extremely popular, book 3 days in advance.

## Indoor Basketball/Volleyball Court
- **Capacity**: Up to 30 people
- **Rules**: Used for official university matches and open recreation.

## Swimming Pool
- **Hours**: 7 AM to 10 PM
- **Rules**: Swimming caps are mandatory. No diving in shallow areas.
